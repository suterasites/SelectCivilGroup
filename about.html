About Us

Est. Geelong

Earthworks and Concrete Done Right

Select Civil Group is a Geelong-based civil construction company proudly servicing the Bellarine Peninsula and surrounding regions throughout Victoria. We specialise in a comprehensive and wide range of civil, concrete, and excavation works, consistently delivering reliable, high-quality results across diverse residential, commercial, and industrial projects of all sizes. With our experienced team and commitment to excellence, we are dedicated to bringing your vision to life with precision and professionalism.

The Work That Holds Up Everything Else

Retaining walls, concrete slabs, driveways, and site cuts demand precision and experience to ensure structural integrity and longevity. We handle factory tilt panels, concrete seating, and comprehensive excavation work with the same meticulous attention to detail and craftsmanship, whether the job is a straightforward residential driveway or a large-scale commercial infrastructure project. Our team brings years of expertise to every aspect of concrete construction and site preparation, guaranteeing that each project meets the highest standards of quality and safety, regardless of scope or complexity.
