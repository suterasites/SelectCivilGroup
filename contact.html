Where We Work

Select Civil Group is a locally owned and operated civil contracting business based in Geelong, Victoria. We provide earthworks, concrete, and excavation services across the greater Geelong region, including the Bellarine Peninsula, Torquay, Ocean Grove, Barwon Heads, Drysdale, Leopold, Lara, and surrounding towns. If your project is in the region, there is a good chance we can help.

We work with homeowners planning residential builds, renovations, and property improvements. We also work alongside builders and contractors on commercial and industrial projects. No matter the size or scope, we bring the same level of professionalism and attention to detail to every job.

Not sure if your location is within our service area? Give us a call or send through an enquiry and we will let you know. We are always happy to have a conversation about your project before committing to anything.

What to Expect

From your first enquiry through to project completion, here is how we work with you. We keep things straightforward so you always know where your project stands.

01

Enquiry

Reach out by phone, email, or through the form on this page. Tell us what you need and we will arrange a time to talk through the details. There is no cost and no obligation at this stage.

02

Site Assessment

We visit your site to assess the conditions, take measurements, and understand the full scope of the work. This helps us give you an accurate quote and realistic timeline for your project.

03

Detailed Quote

You receive a clear, itemised quote with no hidden costs. We break down the work involved so you know exactly what you are paying for. We are happy to answer any questions before you commit.

04

Project Delivery

Once approved, we schedule the work, arrive on time, and get the job done right. We keep you informed throughout the process and leave the site clean when we are finished.
