Earthworks & Excavation

Site preparation, land clearing, precision excavation, and environmental services to get your project started on solid ground across Geelong and the Bellarine Peninsula.

Ground Up

Every construction project starts with the ground. Select Civil Group provides earthworks and excavation services across Geelong, the Bellarine Peninsula, Torquay, and surrounding regions. From clearing raw land and cutting building platforms through to precision trenching for services and footings, we handle the site preparation work that sets the foundation for everything that follows. Our operators work with modern machinery, laser-guided grading equipment, and years of experience in local soil conditions to deliver accurate, efficient results on every job.

Earthworks

Every construction project starts with the ground. Select Civil Group provides earthworks services across the Geelong and Bellarine region, including land clearing, grading, levelling, bulk cut and fill, and soil compaction. Whether you are preparing a single residential lot or a multi-stage subdivision, we have the machinery and the experience to get the ground right.

Proper earthworks are the foundation of everything that follows. Poorly prepared ground leads to drainage problems, foundation issues, and costly rework down the line. We use laser-guided grading equipment and work to engineering specifications to ensure your site is prepared correctly the first time, with spoil removal and disposal handled as part of the service.

Site Cuts

Site cuts create the level building platform needed before any construction can begin. Select Civil Group performs precision site cuts for new homes, extensions, sheds, and commercial buildings throughout Geelong and the surrounding region. We cut to the exact levels specified by your engineer, ensuring the site is ready for footings and slabs without delays.

Our operators work with modern excavation equipment and laser levels to achieve accurate, consistent results. We handle the full scope of work including topsoil stripping, cut and fill balancing, compaction, and spoil removal. If your block has a slope, a site cut is the first step to turning it into a buildable surface, and getting it right at this stage saves time and money on everything that follows.

Detail Excavation

Detail excavation is the precise digging work required for footings, trenches, service trenches, stormwater lines, and other below-ground structures. Select Civil Group performs detail excavation on residential and commercial sites across Geelong, working to engineering drawings and specifications to deliver accurate, clean results.

We use a range of excavators suited to tight-access sites and confined spaces, ensuring we can get the job done efficiently without damaging surrounding structures, landscaping, or services. Detail excavation demands accuracy, and our operators have the experience to dig to exact depths, widths, and profiles specified by your engineer or builder.

Removal of Excess Oil

Excess oil on construction and industrial sites can create environmental hazards, compromise soil integrity, and cause compliance issues. Select Civil Group provides professional removal of excess oil from sites across the Geelong and Bellarine region, ensuring your site meets environmental and safety standards before the next phase of work begins.

We handle the assessment, removal, and responsible disposal of excess oil and contaminated materials in accordance with EPA Victoria guidelines. Whether the oil is from machinery spillage, underground tank leaks, or previous industrial use, we work efficiently to remediate the area so your project can proceed without delays or regulatory complications.
