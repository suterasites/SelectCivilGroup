Earthworks and Concrete Done Right

Select Civil Group is a Geelong-based civil construction company proudly servicing the Bellarine Peninsula and surrounding regions throughout Victoria. We specialise in a comprehensive range of civil, concrete, and excavation works, consistently delivering reliable, high-quality results across diverse residential, commercial, and industrial projects of all sizes.

Retaining walls, concrete slabs, driveways, and site cuts demand precision and experience to ensure structural integrity and longevity. We handle factory tilt panels, concrete seating, and comprehensive excavation work with the same meticulous attention to detail and craftsmanship, whether the job is a straightforward residential driveway or a large-scale commercial infrastructure project.

We work closely with builders, developers, and project managers throughout every phase of construction to keep every job on track and moving forward smoothly. From initial site preparation and foundation work through to the final finished concrete installations, we handle all the important details that separate good work from work that truly lasts.
