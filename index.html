Est. Geelong

Built on Hard Work

Select Civil Group is a locally owned and operated civil contracting business based in Geelong, Victoria. Founded by Andrew Sterling, we specialise in earthworks, concrete services, and site preparation for residential and commercial projects across the Bellarine Peninsula, Torquay, and surrounding regions.

With over a decade of hands-on experience in the industry, our team brings the knowledge, equipment, and work ethic needed to get jobs done right. Whether you are a homeowner looking to pour a new driveway, a builder needing site cuts for a new build, or a developer planning a larger-scale project, we have the capability and commitment to deliver.

We pride ourselves on clear communication, reliable timelines, and quality workmanship. Every job, large or small, gets the same attention to detail. That is the standard we hold ourselves to, and it is why our clients keep coming back.
